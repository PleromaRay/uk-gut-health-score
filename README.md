# UK Gut Health Score  
**NDNS-powered tool to tackle the UK's gut health crisis**  

[![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/Python-3.8%2B-blue)](https://www.python.org/)
[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-green.svg)](LICENSE)

A simple, scientifically grounded Python CLI tool that calculates a **Gut Health Score (0–100)** using real data from the **UK National Diet and Nutrition Survey (NDNS)** and gives personalised, evidence-based recommendations.

Built by **Racheal** – MSc Food Sciences (Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, 2024 )  

### Why this matters
- Only **<10%** of UK adults meet the 30 g/day fiber recommendation  
- **54%** of energy comes from ultra-processed foods (NDNS)  
- Low fermented food & polyphenol intake → poor microbiome diversity → higher risk of obesity, diabetes, IBD, and mental health issues  

This tool translates complex nutrition science into an accessible public-health intervention.
